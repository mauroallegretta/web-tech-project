Idea

During my Erasmus I always meet people from all over the world. This is personally one of the best aspect of the project. Durign a lazy afternoon I started making a list of all the countries that I "visited" just talking with the people. I was really surprised of how far I travelled so I decided to record every single step that I make in the world. This website is my own personal collection. Each name reminds me a country and viceversa so it's impossible to forget them!
